Questions and Answers

Just a few questions for your curious mind.

Why Computer Science?

It blows my mind with what you are able to accomplish when in the driver seat, writing the programs. To add to this, I enjoy the freedom of being able to do something in a way that suits me and having the power of being in control, making the technology do as I program.

What do you aspire for?

Watching Star Trek as a kid, I fell in love with the idea of a Holodeck and virtual reality. Now I aspire to not only enter the virtual reality industry, but also find that merger between virtual reality and gaming and break it wide open. I want to develop an immersive room that places you directly in the center of a game, is fully interactive and has the same feel as that from the Holodeck.

What is your favorite quote?

  • I am who I am, nothing I am going to do to change it.
  • One ultimate goal that a man pursues is to conquer the world. To conquer the world is to reach a new height in life.

What bores you?

What gets you out of bed?

Knowing that every day is a day of its own, I make it a personal goal to be able to learn or at least think about one new thing outside of my normal spectrum. By normal spectrum I mean outside of what I study. For example, while typing this up, today I thought about force fields and how scary they might actually be in real life.
